Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 15, 2025

1. Your Data is Yours

Consumption Tracker is designed with a "local-first" philosophy. By default, all data you enter (daily limits, drink counts, dates) is stored locally on your device using localStorage. This data never leaves your browser unless you explicitly choose to sign in.

2. Cloud Sync (Optional)

If you choose to create an account and sign in, your data will be synchronized with Google Firebase. This allows you to access your history across multiple devices. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising purposes.

3. No Tracking

We do not use third-party analytics cookies or tracking pixels. The application is designed to be a private tool for personal self-improvement.

4. Deletion

You can clear your local data at any time by clearing your browser cache. If you have a cloud account and wish to have your data permanently deleted, please contact us.
